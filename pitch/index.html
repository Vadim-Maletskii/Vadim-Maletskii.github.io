ZeroDepo

Housing without deposit

The problem

• Renting an apartment usually requires a large upfront deposit
• For many people, especially students and young professionals, this is a serious financial barrier
• Deposits are often returned late or partially withheld
• At the same time, deposits do not fully protect landlords from major damage

Why deposits don’t really work

• A standard deposit rarely covers serious damage (fire, water damage, vandalism)
• Tenants freeze a large amount of money for months or years
• Moving to another apartment means finding new liquidity before the old deposit is returned
• The system is inefficient for both sides

The idea

ZeroDepo replaces the traditional deposit with property insurance

• No large upfront payment for tenants
• Full protection for landlords
• Transparent, documented condition of the apartment

How it works (landlord side)

• Our team visits the apartment
• Full inventory of furniture and appliances
• Photos, 3D scans, optionally video
• Data is transferred to an insurance partner
• Insurance price is calculated based on real risk

How it works (tenant side)

• No deposit payment
• Insurance cost is spread across 12 months
• Insurance fee is included in monthly rent
• Lower financial pressure when moving
• Clear and predictable rules

Extra protection layer

• Insurance cost is slightly rounded up

• The difference covers minor damages

Target users (initial focus)

• Students
• Young professionals
• International tenants
• People who move frequently
• Anyone who struggles with upfront costs

(The model itself is scalable beyond this group)

Current project state

• Website concept designed and implemented
• Welcome, host and not available pages
• Listings pages and individual property pages
• Multilingual structure (EN / IT)
• Visual identity direction defined

What comes next (technical)

• Database instead of static listings
• Automated listing generation
• Interactive map on listings page
• Real filtering and search logic
• Booking and host request systems

Why ZeroDepo makes sense

• Solves a real and common problem
• Reduces financial stress for tenants
• Improves security for landlords
• Modernizes an outdated rental practice
• Works even without radical market changes
